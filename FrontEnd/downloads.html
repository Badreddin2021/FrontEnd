These downloads contain two files, one for the species database and one for the bibliography. The formats (fields) are as follows:

Species database:

  • Higher taxa (family, subfamily, order etc.)
  • genus
  • species
  • author
  • year
  • parentheses (Y/N for author, year combination)
  • species - author - year combination
  • synonyms*
  • subspecies*
  • common names*
  • Distribution*
  • comments*
  • Types*
  • Links*
  • Reference numbers*

Bibliography file:

  • Reference number (this links to the Reference numbers in the taxonomy file)
  • author
  • year
  • Title
  • Source
  • URL

*these fields may have multiple lines (line feeds)
