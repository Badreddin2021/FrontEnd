THE SCIENTIFIC ADVISORY BOARD (SAB) OF THE REPTILE DATABASE

The SAB will advise the Reptile Database on general strategic decisions but also on controversial taxonomic issues. We are consulting the SAB a few times a year. Taxonomic issues concerning species or genera typically will be discussed with experts in those areas. The SAB will also be consulted in areas such as taxonomic vandalism or questions of ethical concern such as publication practices. Currently the following people are members of the SAB:
